ABOUT THE GAME

You have to do an action while imitating an animal, a person or an object that are generated randomly as pairings while other players try to guess both of the words!

RULESET

  1. The player who is doing the challenge can see the word pairing.
  2. The player has to do the action while imitating the subject, it is not allowed to do the imitation and the action separately.
  3. The guessing players have to say both the subject and the action to make a correct guess.
  4. The playing order can be decided among the players.

Settings

  1. You can see the available subjects and actions that are currently available in the game in their respective tabs.
  2. You can add your own words to the game by typing them into the box and pressing the submit button.
  3. You can remove the items in the list by clicking on the word you wish to remove.
